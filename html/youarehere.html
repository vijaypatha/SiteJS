Vocabulary to KickOff

Code: You write Code. Code tells the computer what to do. aka Program

Syntax: Computer need a specific set of instruction(code) to do the job. This specifity is called syntax. Imagin your english courses in shool: comma, semi-colon, colon etc.

Statement:Group of symbols come togather as Marvel's Avengers to perform a action or specific task. Just remember: Statement = Avengers and almost-all statement ends with semi-colan(;).

Expression: Statements are made up of expressions

These are good for now...We will increase our deposits in our Vocab bank as we explore more.

Statements and Expression

2 + 2 = 4;    // 2 and 2 came togather with + to perform addition
a - 4 = b;   // a and 4 came with - to perform subtraction
